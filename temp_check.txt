          <p>Archetypal Elements in the Story</p>
          <ul>
            <li>
              The Snake as the Villain - The snake represents danger, fear and
              destruction. Like many villains in stories, it threatens the lives
              of the innocent and creates constant harm.
            </li>
            <li>
              The Crow as the Hero - Even though the crow is not powerful, it is
              determined to protect its family. This makes the crow a “hero”
              figure someone who refuses to give up despite suffering losses.
            </li>
            <li>
              The Jackal as the Wise Helper - The jackal plays the role of the
              guide or mentor. He gives the crows the advice they need when they
              feel lost and hopeless.
            </li>
